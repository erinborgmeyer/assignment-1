about

Born and bred on the beaches of Sydney, Australia, Insight is a clothing brand that has turned heads internationally over the past few years with its dope collections, which include tee shirts, hats, jackets, sweaters, pants, and more. With a central office located in the heart of Sydney, Insight's team stays close to the surf and skate scenes that play a large role in inspiring their designs. Originally launched as a surfboard brand, Insight has evolved over the years into a full-scale fashion boutique.

Insight is a fashion lead surf label. They come from a surf heritage yet lead a lifestyle that encompasses all that is surf, skate, street, art, music, fashion, and popular culture. Insight’s clothing designers follow their intuition to create unique surf fashion, and pay little attention to where the crown is heading – they prefer to create their own path. This isn’t high science of course, however good ideas and good design is priceless and that’s what Insight is all about.
